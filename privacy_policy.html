Privacy Policy for Whimsy

Last updated: August 30, 2025

Introduction

Whimsy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Audio Recordings

Our app may record audio when you use the voice recording features for mental health and well-being purposes. This includes:

  • Voice recordings for reflection and journaling
  • Audio notes and personal memos
  • Voice-based interactions within the app

How We Use Your Information

Audio recordings are used to:

  • Provide gamified journalling experience with sentiment analysis via transcription (speech to text)
  • Enable voice-based journaling and reflection
  • Improve your user experience within the app

Data Storage and Security

Your audio recordings are:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Not saved on external servers without your explicit consent
  • Protected using industry-standard security measures
  • Only accessible by you through the app interface

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal audio recordings with third parties. Your data remains private and under your control.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your audio recordings at any time through the app
  • Control which features have access to your microphone
  • Request information about your data
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Data Retention

Audio recordings are retained until you choose to delete them. You can delete individual recordings or all recordings through the app settings.

Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required for voice recording features
  • Storage: To save your recordings locally on your device

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
